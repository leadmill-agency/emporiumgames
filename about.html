How It Started

A proper gaming hall

Emporium Games was founded by a group of tabletop gamers tired of playing in cramped back rooms and noisy bars. We wanted a dedicated space — somewhere with proper tables, good lighting, a real kitchen, and an atmosphere that made game night feel like an event.

We chose Houston because the city deserved a proper gaming hall. Not a store with a few tables. Not a bar with a shelf of games. A full-service venue built from the ground up for players, parties, and late-night sessions.

The Difference

More than a game store

Today, Emporium Games is where Houston comes to play. From competitive tournaments to birthday parties, from first dates to corporate team-building, we've become the neighborhood spot for anyone who believes the best nights happen around a table.

Our staff are gamers first. They know the rules, they'll teach you, and they'll probably beat you. The kitchen is real — tavern-inspired plates, craft beers, mead flights. And we stay open late because the best games always run longer than you planned.
